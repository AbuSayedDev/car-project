Frequently asked questions

  • How do I get approved to drive?

    How do I get approved to drive?

    To book a car you must create a Turo account, be 18 years old or older with a valid driver’s license, and get approved to drive on Turo.

  • Do I need a US license to drive in the US?

    Do I need a US license to drive in the US?

    No, you don’t need a US license to book a car in the US. Most international driver’s licenses are accepted in the US, Canada, Germany, and the UK.

  • What if I have to cancel my trip?

    What if I have to cancel my trip?

    You can cancel and get a full refund up to 24 hours before your trip starts. If you book a trip with less than 24 hours’ notice, you have one hour after booking to cancel free of charge. If you cancel after the free cancellation period ends, you will be charged a small cancellation fee.

  • What happens if I have an accident?

    What happens if I have an accident?

    If there’s an emergency or an issue with the car, call Turo emergency roadside assistance, available 24/7. We’ll make sure you’re safe, then help you get back on your way

  • Can other people drive a car that I booked?

    Can other people drive a car that I booked?

    Yes, multiple guests can drive the car you book on Turo, as long as they are all approved to drive. The primary driver (whoever booked the car) can add additional drivers before or during a trip for no additional cost.

  • What if a host cancels my trip?

    What if a host cancels my trip?

    In the rare event a host cancels, you’ll be notified immediately so you can book another car, or we’ll help you find one. Your refund can be temporarily held to expedite rebooking, or the funds can be returned to your bank account — your choice.

  • How does Turo Go work?

    How does Turo Go work?

    With Turo Go, you can book, locate, and unlock cars right from the Turo app, without having to meet your host. Simply filter your search for Turo Go-enabled cars and book the personal car rental alternative you want. When it’s time to start your trip, use the Turo app to locate the car and unlock the doors with a tap.
